# Official Sources — VoteWise Reference

## Primary Official Sources

### Election Commission of India (ECI)
- Main portal: https://eci.gov.in
- Voter registration and services: https://voters.eci.gov.in
- Electoral search: https://electoralsearch.eci.gov.in
- Election results: https://results.eci.gov.in
- SVEEP (voter awareness): https://ecisveep.nic.in
- Voter Helpline: 1950

### Government of India
- National Portal: https://india.gov.in
- Ministry of Law and Justice: https://legislative.gov.in
- Aadhaar services: https://uidai.gov.in

### Parliament
- Lok Sabha: https://loksabha.nic.in
- Rajya Sabha: https://rajyasabha.nic.in
- Parliament portal: https://www.sansad.in

### State Election Commissions
- Each state has its own Election Commission for local body elections
- Links can be found at https://eci.gov.in/state-election-commission/

## What VoteWise Uses These For
- Voter registration guidance: voters.eci.gov.in
- Electoral roll verification: electoralsearch.eci.gov.in
- Election schedule and results: eci.gov.in, results.eci.gov.in
- Party recognition data: eci.gov.in/political-parties/
- Legal and constitutional references: legislative.gov.in

## What VoteWise Does NOT Do
- Does not scrape real-time news as election facts
- Does not use unofficial blogs, social media, or political party websites as the truth for legal procedures
- Does not claim to directly verify a user's voter registration status
- Does not provide specific current election dates (as these change every election cycle)
- Always directs users to eci.gov.in for current and official information
