# Indian Election Timeline — Phases

## Overview
Indian general elections (Lok Sabha) follow a structured multi-phase schedule managed by the Election Commission of India. State elections follow a similar but shorter pattern.

## Phase 1: Pre-Election Preparation
- Revision of Electoral Rolls
- Summary Revision periods (usually October-November before an election year)
- Booth Level Officers (BLOs) visit homes to verify voter data
- Special Camp days for adding/correcting voter information

## Phase 2: Election Announcement
- ECI announces the Model Election Schedule
- Dates for notification, last day for nominations, scrutiny, withdrawal, polling, and counting
- The President of India issues the notification for Lok Sabha elections
- The Governor issues the notification for State Assembly elections
- Model Code of Conduct (MCC) comes into effect immediately

## Phase 3: Nomination and Campaign Phase
- Candidates file their nomination papers with the Returning Officer
- Scrutiny of nominations by Returning Officer
- Last date for withdrawal of candidature
- Formal election campaign begins
- Campaigning must stop 48 hours before the first hour of polling

## Phase 4: Polling Day
- Polling stations open at 7:00 AM and close at 6:00 PM (timing can vary by area)
- Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) deployed for security
- Polling is often staggered over multiple dates for large states

## Phase 5: Post-Polling and Counting
- EVMs and VVPAT machines are sealed and stored in strong rooms under CRPF guard
- Counting Day is announced in advance
- Counting agents of candidates are present throughout
- Results are uploaded live on results.eci.gov.in

## Phase 6: Government Formation
- After result declaration, President/Governor invites the largest party or coalition to form government
- Confidence vote (majority of seats in the house) must be proved

## Note on Current Dates
Election dates change every election cycle. Always verify current schedules at:
- https://eci.gov.in
- https://results.eci.gov.in
- Official ECI Press Releases

VoteWise does not store or display current live election dates, as these change frequently and must be taken only from official ECI sources.
