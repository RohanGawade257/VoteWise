# Party Directory Notes — National Political Parties of India

## VoteWise Policy on Party Information
VoteWise provides ONLY factual, official, and neutral information about political parties.
We do NOT:
- Rank parties by performance
- Praise or criticize any party
- Compare parties against each other on merit
- Use party colors in our design
- Recommend any party to voters

## How Parties are Recognized
The Election Commission of India classifies parties as:
- **National Party**: Must win 2% of Lok Sabha seats from 3 states OR get 6% votes in 4 states and win 4 seats OR be recognized in 4 states as a State Party
- **State Party**: Meets ECI recognition criteria in specific states only
- **Registered Unrecognized Party**: Registered with ECI but not yet meeting recognition thresholds

## Recognized National Parties (as of last ECI update)
Note: Party recognition status changes periodically. Verify at eci.gov.in.

### Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)
- Founded: 2012
- Election Symbol: Broom
- Status: National Party
- Background: Emerged from the 2011 anti-corruption movement
- Stated focus: Anti-corruption, governance, public welfare
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

### Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)
- Founded: 1984
- Election Symbol: Elephant
- Status: National Party
- Background: Founded to represent Bahujan communities
- Stated focus: Social equality, Ambedkarite ideology
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

### Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
- Founded: 1980
- Election Symbol: Lotus
- Status: National Party
- Background: Originated from Bharatiya Jana Sangh
- Stated focus: Integral humanism, cultural nationalism, development
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

### Communist Party of India (Marxist) — CPI(M)
- Founded: 1964
- Election Symbol: Hammer, Sickle and Star
- Status: National Party
- Background: Formed following a split from the Communist Party of India
- Stated focus: Marxism-Leninism, workers' rights, secularism
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

### Indian National Congress (INC)
- Founded: 1885
- Election Symbol: Hand
- Status: National Party
- Background: Played a central role in the Indian independence movement
- Stated focus: Secularism, democratic socialism, inclusive growth
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

### National People's Party (NPP)
- Founded: 2013
- Election Symbol: Book
- Status: National Party
- Background: Founded to represent North-East India
- Stated focus: Tribal rights, North-Eastern development
- Source: https://eci.gov.in

## Important Notes
- Leadership of parties changes. Always verify current leadership at official party websites or eci.gov.in.
- VoteWise does not display current leaders to avoid outdated information.
- Party positions and policies change over time. Refer to official manifestos for current positions.
